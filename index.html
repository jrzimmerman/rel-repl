Switching gears:
Lets talk about causal consistency
Lock Step Syncronization
strict linearization
All players sync on each tick
Strongest possible model of consistency
Unless your building a card game,
this kind of sucks.
Dead Reckoning
eventual consistency
Apply inputs immediately
Predict remote events
correct errors
